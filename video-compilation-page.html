The World of Pokemon

On this page, I'll talk about three major ways people interact with this franchise. These aren't all of them, but as per the requirements of this project, I'm only adding 3.

Video Games

This is probably the most popular, and the main, way to interact with the franchise. You can play competitively, once for the experience, shiny hunt, and do so much more.

Trading Card Game

This is a popular way to interact with the franchise as well. Majority of people in this regard are collectors, but there is a small but dedicated community of players too, including myself.

Anime

While maybe not as mainstream as the other two, the anime remains a popular and consistent branch of the franchise, especially amongst younger individuals.
